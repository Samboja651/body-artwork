Tattoo Services for Minors

In compliance with Florida State Law, you must be 18 years or older with a valid photo ID to receive tattoo services without parental involvement.

If you are under 18, you must bring both:

  • A valid photo ID
  • A notarized Minor Consent Form signed by a parent or legal guardian

The form must be completed and signed in front of a notary, and it must include a notary stamp. You can download the required form directly from the Florida Department of Health here:

👉 Minor Consent Form – Florida Department of Health

Please contact Us directly if you have any questions about age verification or the consent process.
